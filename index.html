About Our Institution

A.M. Technical Training Academy (AMTTA) has established since from 1999. Primarily AMTTA started various training activities from Bauphal, Patuakhali The training institution is promoting on sectoral training and accordance with the 11 trades has approved by Bangladesh Technical Education Board-BTEB .

Aims And Objectives Of The Organization

  • Empowerment of women and girls through skill development training and credit support.

  • Ensure social and economical support of person with disability through provision of vocational training and rehabilitation.

  • Livelihood Skills Training of “Strengthen Family Livelihood Skills/Business” to urban working Children.

  • Small and micro enterprise development for social and economical uplift of poor marginal.
